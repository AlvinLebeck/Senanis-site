A practice of reflection

Where will six words take you?

This is a personal space for exploring creative ideas that blend writing, technology, and reflection.

A Moment is a fleeting thought or observation distilled into six words — a glimpse of life and an invitation to pause.

Drawn from senary (six), manan (reflection), and -is (one who does), the word Senanis expresses the idea that six words can hold depth, meaning, and resonance beyond their brevity.

Senanis is a practice of reflection, shaped by attention and constraint. Each Moment is both a glimpse outward, a reflection of the world, and a turning inward, an opportunity to reflect for yourself.

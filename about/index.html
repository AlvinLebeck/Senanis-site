About Senanis

Senanis is a creative project built around six-word Moments: brief reflections that invite attention, interpretation, and pause.

I created senanis as a way to explore how constraint can open meaning. A Moment is small, but not simple. Six words can hold an image, a feeling, a question, or more than one meaning at once. This site brings those Moments into different forms, including weekly arrangement, shared fields of alignment, and quantum sequences of reading.

I'm Alvin R. Lebeck. My work and interests span technology, ideas, and creative exploration, and Senanis grows from that intersection. It is a personal space for experimenting with reflection, language, structure, and interaction.

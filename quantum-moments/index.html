Quantum Moments

Each Moment holds many meanings.

Reading them together invites you to find the one that resonates. These are Quantum Moments.

Kneading reveals the taste of potential.

Joyfully seeing it bloom from within.

Growing size fuels its further expansion.

Scaling beyond normal, thinning edges appear.

Shards and fragments document the end.

Glimpses of something difficult to see.

Tiny specks flicker with the possible.

Unchoreographed motes in display of grandeur.

Existing but without well defined edges.

The space was larger than thought.

Quietly struggling in the dense darkness.

Every direction is lost in nothing.

Groping for a way in panic.

Unable to see doesn't mean invisible.

The possibility of a way forward.

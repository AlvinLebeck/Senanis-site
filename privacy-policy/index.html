Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 13, 2026.

Senanis is a personal creative website. It does not ask visitors to create accounts or submit personal information in order to read or use the interactive experiences currently published here.

What the site stores

Momentle and Moment Field experiences may store small pieces of local browser state on your device so the current week’s puzzle, reveal status, or your in-progress arrangement can persist across page reloads.

What the site does not collect

Senanis does not use these interactive features to collect personal information, build user profiles, or sell data. Any local browser state remains in your own browser unless you clear it.

Hosting and analytics

The site may be served through GitHub Pages and related infrastructure. Standard server logs or platform-level operational data may be processed by those providers as part of hosting the site.

Changes

This policy may be updated as the site evolves. Material changes will be reflected on this page.

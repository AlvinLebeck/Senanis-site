Privacy Policy

What the site stores

Momentle and Moment Field experiences may store small pieces of local browser state on your device so the current week's puzzle, reveal status, or your in-progress arrangement can persist across page reloads.

What the site does not collect

These interactive features are not used to collect personal information, build user profiles, or sell data. Any local browser state stays in your own browser unless you clear it.

This site is served through GitHub Pages and related infrastructure. It does not use browser analytics, tracking scripts, or advertising tags. Standard server logs or platform-level operational data may still be processed by hosting providers such as GitHub as part of delivering the site.

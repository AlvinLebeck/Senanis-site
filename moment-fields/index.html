Moment Fields

Shared structures of alignments

A Moment Field is a finite structure formed from six six-word moments. Together, these moments create a shared space of alignment. Words can be re-aligned to form new moments; no words are added or removed, only their associations change.

Each field stands alone, inviting exploration through interaction.

Moment Fields

Shared structures of possible alignments

A Moment Field is a finite structure formed from six six-word moments. Taken together, these moments define a shared space of possible alignments. Words can be re-aligned to form new moments; no words are added or removed, only their associations change.

Each field stands alone, inviting exploration through interaction.
